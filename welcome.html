Welcome to Deadline Dynamo

Introduction

Deadline Dynamo is a powerful tool that helps you manage your assignments and create efficient plans for your week. With its advanced features, you can streamline your workflow and stay on top of your academic tasks.

Key Features

  • Sync plans across devices (Tested on Chrome, Brave, and Firefox)
  • Extensive customization options in the settings
  • Drag and drop assignments into your daily schedule.
  • See how long assignments might take based on their details.
  • Deadline Dynamo learns from your past performance to improve estimates.
  • Automatic plan creation based on your settings and historical data
  • Notifications for due date changes within planned weeks
  • Works with multiple instances of Canvas at once

Get Started

  1. Head over to your Canvas dashboard.
  2. Click on the 3 dots stacked vertically in the top right area.
  3. Click on "Deadline Dynamo" in the dropdown menu.
  4. Start planning your week with ease!
